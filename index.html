Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025. This policy applies to all applications published by DarkByte Labs on the Google Play Store.

1. Information We Collect

Our apps may collect the following categories of information, depending on the app:

  • Usage data — anonymised analytics to understand feature usage and crash reports.
  • Device identifiers — solely for analytics and advertising (where applicable, as disclosed per app).
  • User-provided content — such as search queries or preferences stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve app performance, stability, and user experience.
  • Serve relevant advertising (where applicable and disclosed).
  • Respond to user support inquiries.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share anonymised, aggregated data with third-party analytics providers (e.g., Firebase) strictly for operational purposes. Any third-party SDKs used are disclosed in the app's Play Store Data Safety section.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

User-generated data stored locally can be cleared by uninstalling the app. For server-side data (if any), contact us at the email below to request deletion within 30 days.

5. Children's Privacy

Apps not rated for children do not knowingly collect data from users under 13. Where our apps are directed at children, they comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google's Families Policy.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS-only data transmission and encrypted local storage where sensitive data is handled.

7. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries: darkbytequantum@gmail.com
