Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 19, 2026  |  Last Updated: June 19, 2026

Welcome to VerseSeeker. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Collect

VerseSeeker is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • No personal data collected: We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information.
  • No account required: You can use the app without creating an account or logging in.
  • Search queries: When you search for a Bible verse, the query is sent to our Bible API to retrieve the verse text. These queries are not stored or linked to your identity.

2. Internet Usage

VerseSeeker requires internet access solely to fetch Bible verse content from a third-party Bible API. This data is used in real time to display verses in the app and is not stored on our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

  • Bible API — Used to retrieve Bible verse text. Please refer to the Bible API's own privacy policy for information on how they handle requests.

We do not use any advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or crash reporting services that collect personal data.

4. Data Storage

VerseSeeker does not store any user data on external servers. Any app preferences or settings (if applicable) are stored locally on your device only.

5. Children's Privacy

VerseSeeker is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data at all, the app is safe for users of any age.

6. Data Security

Because we do not collect or store personal data, there is no user data at risk. All communications with the Bible API are made over secure HTTPS connections.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you have any concerns, please contact us.
